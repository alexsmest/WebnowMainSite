Together we can create new opportunities for your company

Webnow is a new company with new solutions within web development. Webnow was established by Managing Director Alexander Smestad in October 2022. Since then has Webnow employed 12 brilliant web developers who work for clients of Webnow.

We at Webnow are concerned that all companies should have the same opportunities. In order for your company to be visible in today's modern limelight, it requires an extremely high level of expertise or a lot of equity from the company's pocket. Webnow works to make it easier, faster and cheaper for companies to bring in new customers by being more visible on the internet. There are thousands of companies that miss out on potential customers from new fields by not having their own website. Many of these do not have the time, expertise or capital needed to solve the problem. This is where we come into the picture. By using our unique sales and job strategy, we can offer faster results and cheaper services for our customers.


How do we do it?

At Webnow, we have a unique "win" "win" solution for our customers and employees. This solution means that we as employees have the opportunity to build websites for Webnow on the side of our permanent job. Thus, Webnow can have access to a large and intelligent team while maintaining low prices for various services. If none of the employees have the opportunity to take care of a project, the customer is informed immediately of the postponement. Although this has not occurred before, we are well prepared if it were to happen (the delay can vary from a day to a maximum of a week). If we have too many customers and not enough capacity for more, the companies concerned are informed and have the option to cancel the agreement before the project starts.
